Squad. Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us at Squad. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information within the Squad. app.

Information We Collect

We only collect the names you enter within the app. This information is essential for the app's functionality and to provide you with a personalized experience. We do not collect any other personal information about you.

How We Use Your Information

The names you enter are used solely within the app to provide you with a personalized user experience. We do not share or sell your name with any third parties.

Data Storage

We use SwiftData, a secure data storage framework from Apple, to store the names you enter within the app. Your data is encrypted and protected according to Apple's strict privacy standards.

Your Rights

You have the right to review, update, or delete your name within the app at any time. You can do so by accessing the app's settings or contacting us directly.

Security

We take the security of your data seriously. We have implemented robust security measures to protect your name from unauthorized access or disclosure.

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy at any time. We will notify you of any changes through the app or by other means.
